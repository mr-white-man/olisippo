**The Last Signal** is a solo-developed horror experience built in 72 hours for a game jam, then expanded over 3 months into a full release.

## Technical Highlights

- Entire narrative delivered through diegetic interfaces: corrupted terminal text, degraded audio logs, and physical document scanning
- FMOD integration for dynamic audio mixing; environmental sounds procedurally layer based on player location, time of exploration, and discovered lore items
- Custom terminal emulator UI system with simulated command parsing, glitch effects, and time-delayed text reveal
- Atmospheric rendering: volumetric fog via ray-marched shader, film grain and vignette post-processing, flickering light simulation

## From Jam to Release

Started as a 72-hour jam entry that placed 2nd in the Horror category. The core loop — exploring corrupted data to piece together what happened — resonated enough that I spent another 3 months expanding the story, polishing the atmosphere, and shipping it on itch.io where it sits at 4.8/5 stars from 1,200+ ratings.
